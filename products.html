Product 01
SkillVue
Competency Intelligence Platform for Food Manufacturing
Live

SkillVue is Lintejas's flagship product — a fully deployed competency intelligence platform built specifically for the food manufacturing industry. It transforms how organizations identify, develop, and track employee skills across complex production environments.

From HACCP-critical skills to machine operation proficiencies, SkillVue creates a living map of your workforce's capabilities — allowing managers to proactively close gaps, plan training, and ensure compliance readiness at every level.

Key Features

Competency framework builder — define skills, levels, and assessment criteria for each role
Real-time skill gap analysis across teams, departments, and production lines
Training workflow integration — assign, track, and validate training completion
Food safety & compliance tracking with role-specific certification management
Analytics dashboards for workforce readiness, team coverage, and trend reporting
Multi-site support for organizations with multiple production facilities

Material & Consumables Issuance Management System
Precision inventory issuance for manufacturing operations
Coming Soon

A comprehensive materials and consumables issuance management system purpose-built for manufacturing environments. It bridges the gap between procurement, warehouse, and production — creating a fully traceable, auditable chain of custody for every item issued.

Eliminate paper-based issuance logs, prevent unauthorized withdrawals, and gain real-time visibility into consumption rates to drive smarter procurement decisions.

Planned Features

Digital issuance requests with approval workflow and authorization levels
Barcode/QR scanning for fast item identification and issuance logging
Real-time stock level monitoring with automated reorder triggers

Waste and over-issuance analytics for cost reduction insights
Integration-ready for ERP and procurement systems

Product 04
Philippines Transport Navigation System
Intelligent navigation built for Philippine roads & transit
Coming Soon

The Philippines presents unique transportation challenges — congested urban roads, complex multi-modal transit systems (jeepneys, UV express, buses, tricycles), and highly variable traffic conditions. The PH Transport Navigation System is purpose-built for this environment.

Unlike generic navigation tools, this platform incorporates Philippine-specific data: provincial road conditions, fare-based route optimization, local landmark navigation, and multi-modal transit guidance.

Planned Features

Multi-modal route planning — jeepney, bus, UV express, ride-share, and walking combinations
Real-time traffic intelligence with Philippine urban congestion data
Fare estimation across transport modes for budget-conscious route selection
Offline mode for areas with limited connectivity
Local landmark and barangay-level navigation accuracy
Driver and fleet management module for logistics operators

SME Financial Dashboard
Financial clarity and intelligence for small & medium enterprises
Coming Soon

Unified financial overview — cash flow, P&L, balance sheet in one dashboard
