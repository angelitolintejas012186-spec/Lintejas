About Lintejas

A technology company born from a simple belief: great software should solve real problems with precision and elegance — not add complexity.

The Beginning

Built from a Belief
in Better Software

Lintejas was founded in 2026 by Angie — a technologist with a clear conviction: most industries are underserved by software that truly understands their specific workflows, constraints, and people.

Starting with food manufacturing — a sector where workforce competency is mission-critical but often managed through paper, spreadsheets, and institutional memory — Angie built SkillVue, Lintejas's flagship product. SkillVue launched in 2026 and is now live, helping food manufacturers map and develop their workforce's competencies with clarity and precision.

But that was just the beginning. The Lintejas roadmap now includes four additional products spanning manufacturing operations, workforce automation, transport technology, and SME finance — each one targeted at a specific, high-impact problem.

Based in Slovakia, European Union, Lintejas operates with the discipline of a European technology company and the ambition of a global software builder. The goal is simple: build one great product per industry, and do it right.

Mission & Vision

What We Stand For

Mission
To build precision software that transforms how industries operate — one carefully crafted product at a time.

We don't build generic platforms. Every Lintejas product starts with deep research into a specific industry's pain points, workflows, and constraints — then we engineer a solution that fits like a glove. No feature bloat, no one-size-fits-all compromises.

🔭
Vision
A world where every industry has access to enterprise-grade software built specifically for its needs.

From Slovak manufacturing floors to Philippine streets — we believe the best software is hyper-specific, deeply considered, and built by people who genuinely understand the domain.

⭐
Promise
Every product ships only when it's genuinely ready — no half-finished MVPs, no feature theater.

SkillVue took months of focused development before it went live. That standard applies to every product in our pipeline. We'd rather ship late and right than early and wrong.
