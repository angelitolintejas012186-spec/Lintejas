FAQ

Common Questions

Is SkillVue available to use right now?

Yes! SkillVue is live and deployed at competenc.netlify.app. You can access it directly or reach out to us for a guided demo.

When will the other four products be available?

All four remaining products — Material & Consumables, Staff Scheduler, PH Transport Navigation, and SME Financial Dashboard — are currently in development. Register your interest via the contact form and we'll notify you with updates.

Does Lintejas offer enterprise or custom implementations?

Yes. We work with organizations that need customization, integration with existing systems, or dedicated deployments. Use the contact form and select "Enterprise Inquiry" to start that conversation.

Is Lintejas open to investment or partnerships?

Absolutely. We welcome conversations with aligned investors and strategic partners. Use the contact form with "Investment" or "Partnership Opportunity" selected and we'll schedule a call.

Where is Lintejas based and what data standards do you follow?

Lintejas is headquartered in Slovakia, European Union. We build all products with GDPR compliance and EU data sovereignty standards as first-class requirements — not afterthoughts.

Can I get early access to products that are coming soon?

Yes. We're selectively accepting early-access partners for our upcoming products. Reach out via the contact form and indicate which product you're interested in and your use case.
